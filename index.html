Character Select · Introduction

PC
LV. Research Scientist

I am a Research Scientist at the University of Wisconsin–Madison, working with Prof. Daifeng Wang. I develop deep learning methods and computational tools for multi-modal integration, focusing on personalized functional genomics and the molecular mechanisms underlying various diseases.

I hold a PhD in Biomedical Informatics from Arizona State University, where I worked on fine-mapping functional noncoding genetic elements using machine learning, and an MS in Computer Science, also from ASU.
