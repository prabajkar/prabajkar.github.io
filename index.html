About

Card 01 / Introduction

JE

I'm a researcher working at the intersection of ecological modeling and machine learning, currently based at the Cascade Institute. My work focuses on predicting how species distributions shift under environmental change, using tools from spatial statistics and deep learning.

Before this, I completed my PhD at the University of the Pacific Northwest, where I studied population dynamics in freshwater ecosystems. I'm generally interested in building models that are useful to conservation practitioners, not just accurate on a benchmark.
